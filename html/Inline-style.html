MAHABALESHWAR

Mahabaleshwar is also known as the city of strawberries. it is the source of the krishna river.There are many points at Mahabaleshwar which are major tourist attractions.some of them are Arthur's seat, Wilson's Point, kate's Point and Three monkey Point. you can also enjoy boating on the vena lake. Additional attractions are hiking,trekking and cycling.
